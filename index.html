Castles and Ruins in Baden-Württemberg
  • About

  • Baden-Württemnerg is home to hundreds of beautiful historic castles and ruins. This map shows all castles and ruins in Baden Württemberg that are on OpenStreetMap as of 19.07.17 and have a name.

    Click on the castle/ruins icons on the map to add them to the bookmarks list in the sidebar. Clicking on the bookmark entry will zoom to that castle/ruins.
